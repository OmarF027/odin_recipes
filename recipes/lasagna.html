Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian al forno dish that generally consists of alternating layers of pasta sheets and various fillings, including - though not exclusively - ragù, besciamella and cheese, such as mozzarella, ricotta or Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef in a pan and add tomato sauce.
  3. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  5. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, then let it cool before serving.
